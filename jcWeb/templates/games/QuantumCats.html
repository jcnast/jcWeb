Quantum Cats

The kittens have been trapped in boxes stored in physics labs accross the Univeristy of Waterloo's campus! Use the remaining cats, your understanding of Physics, the powers of Quanum Mechanics, and various equipment left within the labs to free them! How high of a score can you get?

Each cat in game has some property of quantum mechanics associated with it, along with a brief explanantion of how the power connects to quantum mechanics. This explanation also mentions how the mechanic is used in game. To learn more about how a certain cat behaves, be sure to check out the information provided about it!

In addition to the cats and quantum elements in the game, there are also objects left over in the labs that can be used to help you free the kittens! The objects that you can use are:

  • Lasers, which will inflict massive damage to objects in front of them once activated
  • Magnets, which will pull in all metal objects within a certain radius
  • Liquid Nitrogen, which makes all objects within a certain radius much more brittle and therefore more easily destroyed

For more information and an analysis of the design/development process, or if you would like to leave any comments about the game, please view the Quantum Cats Post Mortem.

Controls

Everything is touch-based with required touches being signified by pulsing white circles.

Supported Devices

This app is built for Android (4.0 and up) and Apple (iOS 6.0 and up) devices.

Software Used
  • Game Engine: Unity 5
  • Language: C#
