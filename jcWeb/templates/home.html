Overview

This website is a showcase of my ability to develop and design games. I have provided a short Bio and my resume for those who are considering me for employment. Outside of talking about myself, there are two main sections: "Games" and "Analysis". My resume and all games are available for download, when this is the case a link will appear in the header.

The Games section is a showcase of all the games that I have been a part of to date and will focus on the game-development aspect of game creation. The majority of them have been works of passion, completed on my own or in a team at a game jams. The others, that are not available for direct download, have been produced in a professional setting and will redirect you to their respective download pages. I am using this section to showcase the various game engines and software I am familiar with, as well as the coding languages used in their production. New additions are in construction!

My Analysis section is where I will focus on the game-design thought process of game creation. There are some discussions that will be stand-alone and simply touch on arbitrary analysis of games where others will be part of series that have a central theme (ex: Post Mortems). These analysis will range from talking about my own games, various mechanics in games and their impact on the player, to general discussions about games I did not help design, such as what makes a game suitable to be an E-Sport and various decisions made from game companies on games that I am passionate about.
