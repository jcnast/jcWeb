Overview

This app was designed to be a self-guided tour of the Quantum Nano Center on campus at the University of Waterloo. The app works with a combination of iBeacons and NFC chips to guide the participant through the building and unlock the pieces of information spread out within the building. The information was provided by an external source, we simply provided a useable app to integrate and present it.

Summary

  • Visuals have a massive impact on how enjoyable a game can be
  • Relevant information should always be accesible when required
  • Communication is key

Intentions

We wanted this app to be very simple to used, with the focus being primarily on the information being presented and having the users explore the building. All options, and the navigation between all available screens, had to be as clear as possible to reduce any frustration with the app itself. This application was acting purely as an interface for the users to learn about the building and the goings on within.
